## Image prompt

Abstract futuristic blueprint-style artwork on a deep navy background (#1a1a2e), drawn with thin technical lines in light gray (#e0e0e0).

Three rectangular panels arranged side by side, each with different internal wireframe structures — one sparse and centered, one dense with a sidebar, one flowing with horizontal sections. Despite their different layouts, all three share the same border treatment, the same background tone, and small accent marks in identical positions. Faint connecting lines between the panels suggest they belong to one system. The emphasis is on unity across difference — three distinct surfaces, one identity.

Include faint blueprint grid lines and technical construction marks, but keep them minimal and clean.

No text, no labels, no UI elements — purely abstract.

Style: minimal, precise, engineering blueprint aesthetic, high contrast, lots of negative space, strict geometry.

No gradients, no glow effects beyond very subtle line intensity variation.

Mood: calm, systemic, engineered — coherence across surfaces.
