## Image idea

A printing press or publication mechanism rendered as blueprint schematic. Sheets flowing through a structured pipeline, each carrying faint grid patterns. Precise and engineered — publishing as infrastructure.

## Image prompt

Abstract futuristic blueprint-style artwork on a deep navy background (#1a1a2e), drawn with thin technical lines in light gray (#e0e0e0).

A schematic cross-section of a precision mechanism — part printing press, part assembly line. Rectangular sheets move through a structured pipeline of rollers and guides. Each sheet carries faint internal grid patterns representing content structure. The mechanism has clear input and output stages. Geometric precision throughout — engineered publishing, not artisanal craft. A small stack of completed sheets at the output end, neatly aligned.

Include faint blueprint grid lines and technical construction marks, but keep them minimal and clean.

No text, no labels, no UI elements — purely abstract.

Style: minimal, precise, engineering blueprint aesthetic, high contrast, lots of negative space, strict geometry.

No gradients, no glow effects beyond very subtle line intensity variation.

Mood: calm, systemic, engineered — methodical production.
