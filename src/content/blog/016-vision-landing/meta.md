## Image prompt

Abstract futuristic blueprint-style artwork on a deep navy background (#1a1a2e), drawn with thin technical lines in light gray (#e0e0e0).

A horizontal progression of five increasingly complex technical schematics, arranged left to right like an evolutionary timeline. Far left: a minimal rectangular block with a few internal lines — a simple script. Second: a slightly larger structure with interconnected modules — a framework. Third: a more elaborate schematic with branching pathways and nested components — an autonomous runtime. Fourth: a complex architectural blueprint with multiple coordinated subsystems — a team of agents. Far right: the outline of a humanoid figure rendered in the same technical drafting style, its body composed of precise geometric components and connection lines. Each stage is slightly taller and more intricate than the previous. A faint horizontal axis line connects all five stages at their base. Subtle dotted arrows between stages suggest forward movement.

Include faint blueprint grid lines and technical construction marks, but keep them minimal and clean.

No text, no labels, no UI elements — purely abstract.

Style: minimal, precise, engineering blueprint aesthetic, high contrast, lots of negative space, strict geometry.

No gradients, no glow effects beyond very subtle line intensity variation.

Mood: ambitious, forward-looking, engineered.
